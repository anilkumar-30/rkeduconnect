About the Counsellor

Resham Bahadur

18+ Years Experience | CSR Contributor | Student Mentor

Resham Bahadur is a dedicated education counsellor with over 18 years of corporate experience across diverse domains including Banking, Customer Service, Procurement, and IT Service Management. His strong professional background brings a structured, transparent, and result-oriented approach to student guidance and career planning.

Driven by a passion for helping students succeed, Resham has been actively involved in counselling students from government schools as part of CSR initiatives, guiding them towards better education and career opportunities.

What sets him apart is his genuine commitment to student success. He believes that every student deserves the right guidance, regardless of their background. As part of his social responsibility, he contributes 15% of his income towards supporting education and charitable causes.

With a calm, approachable, and practical counselling style, Resham focuses on understanding each student’s strengths, interests, and goals. He provides honest advice, clear direction, and end-to-end support for students aspiring to pursue higher education abroad.
